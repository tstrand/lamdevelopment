Bringing Solar to New York State

Local Action That Benefits Your Community

LAM Development believes solar energy should be available to everyone. We focus on bringing cost-saving renewable power to local municipalities, enabling communities of all sizes to attain energy security.

Through the deployment of smaller, locally based solar farms we are able to provide clean, affordable energy in a responsible way.

We Bring You the Best in the Business

In order to bring solar energy to local communities, we leverage our vast network of partnerships with utilities, construction firms, and real estate companies to quickly and efficiently meet the energy needs of our clients.

LAM Development's knowledge and expertise as the region's premier solar developer allows us to work with the most capable firms in each field and bring together the best practices and results.

Lasting Partnerships For Lasting Results

We understand that residents know what is best for their own municipality. Therefore, we approach each solar project as an opportunity to collaborate with the community. LAM Development works to understand the unique needs and desires of all stakeholders, and maximize the positive results for everyone involved. We are here to listen, learn, and help craft the best possible solar solution.
