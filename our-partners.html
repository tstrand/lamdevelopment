Take Part in the Solar Revolution

LAM Development offers landowners a variety of ways to monetize their land, including lease and purchase agreements.

Flexibility and insight allow us to meet the specific needs of every landowner. LAM Development's goal is to present individuals with opportunities to generate income for themselves while helping their community benefit from solar.


Through a partnership with LAM Development your land will be used to provide clean, renewable energy for generations to come. Land leased or sold to LAM Development will be used to produce cost-saving solar energy, improve your local community, lower municipal electricity costs, and help bring about a brighter future.

By partnering with LAM Development landowners are not responsible for the construction, operation, and maintenance of the solar facility. LAM Development works to ensure any aesthetic or environmental concerns of the landowners are addresses throughout the lifetime of the solar energy facility. The land we use is returned to its original condition at the end of a land lease.

LAM Development is here for the long-term and values its relationships with local landowners and stakeholders. We listen to the needs and concerns of the community to ensure a project's success from start to finish.

We're here to help

LAM Development helps communities lower their utility bills and energy costs through clean solar energy. Utilizing our expertise, we provide the assistance needed to navigate the steps of a transition to cost-saving renewable solar power.

Working Together Towards a Brighter Future

The switch to solar energy is a choice made by and for local residents. As a leader in solar development, LAM Development strives to foster lasting partnerships with communities.

All of LAM Development’s projects follow three guiding principles:

A Local Focus

We understand that no two municipalities are alike, and that each has its own particular needs and desires. As a result, we design each solar solution to maximize the benefits provided to the community based on local circumstance.


OPEN DIALOGUE WITH THE COMMUNITY

From the initial meeting to the removal of the solar energy facility, LAM Development ensures that local stakeholders are involved every step of the way. We engage all parties and incorporate their feedback to guarantee that all voices are heard.


A Lasting Commitment

LAM Development is here for the long term. We are focused on creating a lasting relationship to guarantee your community’s transition to solar energy is a success for decades to come.

Helping Navigate the Energy Landscape

The energy industry is undergoing rapid change. Technological advancement has revolutionized the energy industry, creating new opportunities for everyone from customers to electricity suppliers.

To ensure the success of our projects, LAM Development works closely with the bedrock of the energy system: Utilities

LAM Development aligns its interests with those of local utility companies, working towards a cleaner, more dynamic grid. By working closely with utilities, we establish a productive and efficient partnership between the local community, the utility, and ourself.

Through solar LAM Development seeks to increase the benefits to both the local and regional grids. Our invaluable relationships to utility companies give us a distinct comparative edge over our competitors, assuring the transition to renewable energy will be simple, seamless, and hassle-free.

LAM Development is a solar development company that focuses on bringing renewable, affordable power to municipalities. We accomplish this by leveraging our vast network with utilities, construction firms, and real estate companies to quickly and efficiently meet the power needs of our clients. Our knowledge and experience as solar developers allows us to bring together the most capable contractors in order to maximize the positive impact on the town and the environment.
